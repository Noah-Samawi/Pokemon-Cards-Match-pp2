How to Play

The goal of the game is to match pairs of cards with identical images.

Cards are randomly shuffled and placed face down on the board at the start of the game.

  1. Players reveal cards by clicking on them.
  2. Clicking on a face-down card reveals its image for a brief moment
  3. Players must remember the locations and images of the revealed cards.
  4. When you have selected all of the cards match the game will end and you will see your score
