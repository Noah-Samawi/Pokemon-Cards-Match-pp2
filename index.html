Instructions

The goal of the game is to match pairs of cards with identical images.

1- Enter your name and press Submit, then press Start for the Cards Match to begin.
2- You have 15 seconds to answer each Cards Match .
3- Timer is displayed on Game area.
4- If you don't answer in the 25 sec's the quiz will end.
5- Once an answer is selected, the correct answer will turn green.
6- It's required to select an answer to move on to the next question.
7- Answer all 10 questions to end quiz
